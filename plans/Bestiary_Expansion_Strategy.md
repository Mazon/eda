# Bestiary Expansion Strategy: The 100-Enemy Goal

To achieve a professional-grade Bestiary with 100+ unique entries while maintaining the dark fantasy tone of *Eda*, we will use a **Categorized Variant System**. This ensures the GM has a wide variety of threats for every environment.

## 1. Classification & Tiers
Each creature should be assigned a **Threat Level** (1-5) and a **Category**.

### Proposed Categories (10-12 per category)
1.  **Wildlife & Beasts**: Wolves, Bears, Spiders, Owls, Boars, Great Bats.
2.  **Outlaws & Desperate Souls**: Bandits, Assassins, Thugs, Rogue Knights.
3.  **The Fanatics**: Zealots, Inquisitors, Cultists, Sacrificial Victims.
4.  **The Hollowed (Mutants)**: Ichor-Bloated, Many-Limbed, Skin-Walkers.
5.  **The Grave-Bound (Undead)**: Skeletons, Zombies, Wraiths, Draugr, Ghouls.
6.  **Ancient Folk (Nature Spirits)**: Trolls, Giants, Moss-Men, Hags.
7.  **Void-Spawn (Eldritch)**: Formless Horrors, Mind-Eaters, Shadow-Leeches.
8.  **Automata & Constructs**: Golems, Animated Statues, Clockwork Watchers.

---

## 2. Scaling via Variants
Instead of writing 100 completely unique stat blocks, we create **Base Archetypes** and apply **Variants**.

**Example: The Wolf Base**
*   **Wolf (Standard)**: Level 1.
*   **Wolf Alpha**: +10 HP, +1 Reaction. (Variant 1)
*   **Ichor-Wolf**: Deals +2 Damage, causes Fear. (Variant 2)
*   **Ghost-Wolf**: Incorporeal, ignores armor. (Variant 3)
*   **Pack-Lord**: Can summon 1d4 Wolves. (Variant 4)

## 4. Implementation Plan
1.  **Develop a Universal Stat Template**: Ensure all 100 entries follow the same clear layout.
2.  **Create "Regional Random Tables"**: At the end of the Bestiary, provide tables (e.g., "Encounters in the Frozen Woods") to help GMs pick from the 100 enemies quickly.
3.  **Include "Monster Tactics"**: For each major archetype, add a 1-sentence "Behavior" note (e.g., "Always attacks the weakest character first").

## 5. Visual Consistency
*   All 100 entries will be presented in **Black & White**.
*   High-level monsters (Threat Level 4-5) will get larger illustrations.
*   Low-level monsters (Threat Level 1) will be grouped on a single page with small sketches.
